Did you know?

Check your voltage first

Total voltage = number of LEDs × voltage per LED. A 16-LED strip at 6V each needs 96V from the driver — order the wrong voltage and the strip won't light.

Match strip length and count

You need both the right number of strips and the right length per strip. A set with the correct total but wrong per-strip length won't seat in your panel.

The same strip fits many TVs

Brands share panels, so one serial can cover several models. If your backlight number matches, the strip fits — even under a different brand name.
